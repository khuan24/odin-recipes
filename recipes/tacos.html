Tacos al Pastor

Description

Prepare your taste buds for this deliciously tender and flavorful taco al pastor recipe. Recipe creator docmancito suggests serving tacos al pastor with warm corn tortillas, tomatillos salsa, lemon or lime wedges, grilled pineapple, chopped onion, and cilantro. Store the tacos al pastor meat separately from the tortillas and toppings. Place the meat in a shallow, airtight container and store in the refrigerator for up to three days. For the best results, reheat the pork on the stove.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Pork
  • Pepper
  • Pineapple
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Roast, peel, and de-seed the tomatoes. Boil and drain the peppers.
  2. Make the marinade in a blender.
  3. Marinate pork in the refrigerator for four hours or up to overnight.
  4. Grill and chop the remaining pineapple slices.
  5. Cook the pork on a grill pan until browned.
  6. Chop and serve the pork.
